Smt.Sulochanadevi Singhania School

The school was founded in 1969 by late Shri. Gopalkrishna Singhania in memory of his wife Smt. Sulochanadevi Singhania. Smt. Sulochanadevi Singhania School is located in lush green surroundings of Jekegram in the heart of the city of Thane. The premier institution was started in 1969 by Shri. Gopalkrishna Singhania. Over the years, it has grown to accommodate children from all walks of life. Today, it has 6400+ students on roll from KG to Std 12. The school is an unaided linguistic minority institution run by the Smt. Sulochanadevi Singhania Trust. Air Cdre. Vijaypat Singhania is the Chairman and has on its Board of Governors, eminent professionals from the field of Management and Education.

Achivements


1 in Maharashtra and No. 1 in Thane in the Educational World India School Rankings 2019-20 survey. The school is ranked India's No. 1 in the following parameters : Co-curricular Education, Internationalism, Teacher Welfare and Development, Value For Money, Sports Education in the Co-ed Day School Category.

Invited as Speaker to address Schools in South France by the Mayor in June.
Appointed District Commissioner, Thane, for Bharat Scouts and Guides by the State Government for the next three years.

Invited panelist for discussion, on improving quality in education, Bloomberg TV
Invited as speaker at the National Conference on Social Sensitivity, Mumbai.
Invited as Speaker to address Principals in Mumbai, Navi Mumbai and Thane district on ‘Better Classroom management and changing Pedagogy’.
The Principal has also been bestowed with the Best Principal Award in the international category for the sixth time in a row
Published articles by invitation, for Hindustan Times on ‘Changing paradigms in Education’
The school has led International Curriculum Exchange Programmes with schools in UK, Holland, Denmark, France, Germany & Canada. Students and faculty exchanges are regular features of the curriculum programme.
